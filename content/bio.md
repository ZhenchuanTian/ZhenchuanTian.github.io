I am a PhD candidate at the [Catalan Institute of Nanoscience and Nanotechnology (ICN2)](https://icn2.cat), supervised by [Prof. Monica Lira-Cantú](https://icn2.cat/en/nanostructured-materials-for-photovoltaic-energy-group/monica-lira-cantu). My research focuses on engineering high-efficiency perovskite solar cells and all-perovskite tandem architectures, with particular emphasis on bulk and interficial engineering, and long-term operando stability characterisation.

Prior to my PhD, I completed a **Master of Multidisciplinary Research in Experimental Sciences** at Universitat Pompeu Fabra (BIST Joint Master Programme, 2023–2024) and a **Bachelor of Engineering in Materials Science and Engineering** at Sichuan University of Science and Engineering, China (2019–2023).

During a research visit at [Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences](https://www.siat.ac.cn/) (Feb–May 2025, supervised by Prof. Zaiwei Wang), I achieved single-junction PCEs exceeding **26%** and contributed to all-perovskite tandem devices surpassing **28%** — approaching state-of-the-art certified performance.
